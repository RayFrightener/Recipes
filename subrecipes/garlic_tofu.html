GARLIC TOFU

Description

This recipe is a spicy and savoury tofu dish. It can be made in 45 minutes and tastes great by itself or brown rice of white rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 block extra firm tofu
  • 5-6 cloves garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 1-2 carrots or any vegetable that you would like to put in it (you can put any you think will taste good)
